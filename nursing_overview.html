Overview

What is it?

Students who attend and graduate from Spruce Nursing are sought after in the marketplace. That’s because Spruce Nursing programs help you develop the specialized knowledge and skills that make you a better candidate for jobs that are in high demand in the medical field.

Our Medical Training Programs Are 100% Medical Focused

Many career training schools offer a range of programs in multiple fields. Because we focus exclusively on healthcare, we provide our students with the specialized knowledge, skills, and training that prepares our graduates for a strong, successful medical career whether they’re enrolled in our practical nursing program or our medical assistant program.

Become a Medical Professional

The indications of quality education are when graduates pass post-graduate examinations entitling them to receive state licenses. At Spruce Nursing, you’ll participate in training that will prepare you for the NCLEX examination needed to work as a Practical Nurse. You’ll take pride in your Spruce Nursing experience when you’ve achieved your educational goals and set off on your new career path.

Supportive Financial Aid

Education is an investment in you. That means what you put into it will grow way beyond what you originally spent. Spruce Nursing takes pride in keeping programs affordable while still offering a high-quality educational experience. You will have everything you need to be successful as quickly as possible so you can get on with your career.

We understand financial challenges and what it takes to get an education while still managing your daily life and family obligations. That’s why financial aid advisors will assist you through the financial aid process and will discuss the payment options available to best meet your budget.

Clinical Training for Practical Nursing Students

Spruce Nursing’s nursing students get clinical training that helps build confidence in the nursing field. Our Practical Nursing program consists of 720 hours of didactic, 120 hours of laboratory, plus 720 hours of clinical training which means more than half the program consists of applied learning.

Medical Career Placement Assistance

Career advisors will help guide you from program to profession. They’ll help you learn how to make a great first impression and be the best candidate you can be with solid interview skills and a résumé you can feel confident about. As a Spruce Nursing graduate, you’ll have access to our vast network of employers and industry professionals who are eager to help you advance your career in your field of choice.
